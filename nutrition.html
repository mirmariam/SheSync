🩸 Menstrual Phase

Focus on: Iron-rich foods, anti-inflammatory foods

  • Leafy greens (spinach, kale)
  • Red meat, lentils, quinoa
  • Dark chocolate (70%+ cacao)
  • Ginger tea for cramps
  • Turmeric for inflammation

Avoid: Processed foods, excessive caffeine, alcohol

🌱 Follicular Phase

Focus on: Fresh, light foods to support rising energy

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Fermented foods (kimchi, yogurt)
  • Lean proteins (fish, chicken)
  • Sprouted seeds and grains
  • Green tea
🥚 Ovulatory Phase

Focus on: Antioxidant-rich foods, fiber

  • Berries and citrus fruits
  • Colorful vegetables
  • Whole grains
  • Coconut and avocado
  • Herbal teas
🌙 Luteal Phase

Focus on: Magnesium, B-vitamins, complex carbs

  • Magnesium-rich foods (pumpkin seeds, almonds)
  • Complex carbohydrates (sweet potato, oats)
  • Calcium sources (dairy, sesame seeds)
  • Mood-supporting foods (bananas, turkey)
  • Chamomile tea for PMS

Limit: Salt, sugar, caffeine to reduce PMS symptoms
